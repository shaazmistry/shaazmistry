## 💫 About Me

I'm **Shaaz** — an MCA student who builds intelligent systems and then makes them *beautiful*. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, DevOps engineering, and graphic design. I believe that data storytelling is most powerful when it's both rigorous and visually compelling.

### 🎓 The Master's Journey

My academic foundation in **Machine Learning** and **Python-based simulations** drives me to architect predictive models that uncover hidden patterns — from classifying celestial spectra in the SDSS to building health data dashboards. Every project is a chance to push neural architectures further.

### 🔧 Evolution into DevOps

Code that works is only half the battle. The real victory lies in **code that scales**. I'm actively mastering containerization via **Docker**, automating workflows through **CI/CD pipelines**, and managing robust **Linux-based** infrastructures.

### 🎨 The Designer's Edge

My **Graphic Design** background means I approach the terminal with the eye of a curator. Whether building a Streamlit interface or designing a data dashboard, I prioritize UI/UX clarity — transforming cold numbers into vibrant, intuitive experiences.
